The Ultimate Guide to Making Mini Momos!

Craving something savory and delicious? These bite-sized mini momos are the perfect treat! Whether steamed or pan-fried, these dumplings are packed with flavor and make a great snack or appetizer. Follow this simple recipe to create soft, juicy momos that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped vegetables (cabbage, carrots, onions)
  • 1/4 cup minced chicken (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour with water to form a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, combine chopped vegetables, minced chicken (if using), soy sauce, salt, pepper, garlic, and ginger paste. Mix well.
  3. Roll the dough into small circles, place a teaspoon of filling in the center, and fold to seal the edges.
  4. Steam the momos for 8-10 minutes until the wrappers turn translucent.
  5. (Optional) Pan-fry for a crispy texture.
  6. Serve hot with spicy dipping sauce and enjoy!
